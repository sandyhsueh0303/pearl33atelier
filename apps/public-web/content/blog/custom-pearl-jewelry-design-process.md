## Why Choose Custom Pearl Jewelry

Custom design gives you full control over proportion, pearl type, and metal tone. It is ideal when you want a meaningful gift, an event piece, or a signature design that is not available in standard collections.

## Step 1: Consultation

We start with your style direction, budget range, and occasion. This helps narrow suitable pearl types, size ranges, and setting options before production.

## Step 2: Pearl Selection

Based on your goals, we prepare pearl options and explain tradeoffs in luster, size, surface, and overtone. You can compare options side by side and choose what looks best in real light.

## Step 3: Design Confirmation

After pearl selection, we confirm design details including measurements, metal color, and finishing style. Once approved, production begins.

## Step 4: Handcrafting and Delivery

Your piece is completed with final quality checks for matching, setting security, and wear comfort. Typical custom timelines are around 3 to 8 weeks depending on complexity and pearl availability.
